## Challenge

**Freedom2hear** (formerly GoBubble) is an AI-powered platform that detects and hides harmful content across text and media by analysing emotion and intent in real time.

I joined at a pivotal moment. The company was in the midst of a complete reset: launching a self-serve moderation platform, repositioning the brand, and scaling its technology. What it lacked was a unified vision, cross-functional alignment, and a clear product–market fit story.

---

## Product Design

### A Self-Serve Content Moderation Platform

When I joined the team, I was tasked with leading the redesign of a new **B2B self-serve content moderation platform for enterprise-level clients.** This included onboarding flows, the moderation hub, reporting tools, and the admin experience.

![Content Moderation Platform](/case-studies/freedom2hear/platform.png)

With a small team and limited budget, we conducted **user interviews and competitive analysis.** Combined with in-depth client knowledge from our Client Success and Sales teams, this helped us define key user types and their needs.

![Research Affinity Mapping](/case-studies/freedom2hear/research-notes.png)

The result was an improved Super Admin experience for better client management, increased efficiency in the moderation hub for manual moderators, and a more intuitive information architecture and UI for social media managers.

## Growth

### Leading Growth Design Initiatives

Working with a creative agency, I led the development of new **brand guidelines, a new marketing website, sales assets, and social media content.**

![Marketing Website](/case-studies/freedom2hear/website.png)

I created a **design system** with shared foundations across product and marketing to streamline design work managed by our small team, ensure consistency and focus on accessibility.

![Figma & Webflow Sync](/case-studies/freedom2hear/variables.png)

### New Product Demo

I led the design and build of a **new product demo in Webflow**. This initiative **removed friction** from the update process, reduced reliance on development, and gave the Sales and Marketing teams greater control. It also enabled content personalisation for prospective clients in sports, media, and gaming.

The new approach has significantly improved **demo quality** and is set to increase **operational efficiency**, ensuring a more agile, client-centric way to showcase our platform.
